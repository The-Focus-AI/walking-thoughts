# Cinematic Shot Examples

Complete prompt examples for different camera movements and shot types.

## Establishing Shots

### City Skyline

```bash
nano-banana --video "Sweeping aerial drone shot over a modern city at golden hour.
Camera glides smoothly above glass skyscrapers, catching the warm sunset light
reflecting off windows. Starts high, gradually descends toward street level.
Traffic visible below, tiny cars moving on highways.

16:9 cinematic aspect ratio. Professional documentary quality.
Ambient sounds: Distant city hum, wind, helicopter in distance.

Smooth stabilized motion throughout. No subtitles, no text overlay."
```

### Natural Landscape

```bash
nano-banana --video "Slow pan right across a mountain range at sunrise.
Snow-capped peaks emerge from morning mist, golden light painting
the summits. Pine forest visible in the valley below.

Epic nature documentary style. 24mm wide-angle lens equivalent.
Majestic, awe-inspiring atmosphere.

Ambient: Mountain wind, distant eagle cry, subtle orchestral swell.

8-second duration covering 90 degrees. No subtitles."
```

## Character Introduction

### Professional Setting

```bash
nano-banana --video "Slow dolly-in from medium to close-up over 8 seconds.

A confident businesswoman in her 40s, silver-streaked dark hair in a
professional updo, navy blazer, pearl earrings. She reviews documents
at her desk, then looks up at camera at 5-second mark with a knowing smile.

Modern corner office, floor-to-ceiling windows showing city view.
Soft natural light from windows, supplemented by warm desk lamp.

Sound effects: Paper shuffling, pen placed down.
Ambient: Quiet office atmosphere, distant typing.

Executive portrait quality. No subtitles, no text overlay."
```

### Casual Setting

```bash
nano-banana --video "Tracking shot from the side following a young artist.

A creative woman in her 20s, colorful paint-stained overalls, curly red hair
in a messy bun, walks through her art studio examining canvases on the walls.
She pauses at one, tilting her head thoughtfully.

Bright studio space with large windows, finished and unfinished paintings
everywhere. Natural afternoon light creating long shadows.

Ambient: Soft indie music playing, paint brushes in water, city sounds outside.

Artistic, documentary feel. No subtitles."
```

## Product Shots

### Food/Beverage

```bash
nano-banana --video "Static shot with subtle dolly-in over 8 seconds.

A perfectly crafted latte sits on a marble countertop. Steam rises gently,
catching the soft morning light. A hand enters frame at 4 seconds,
lifting the cup with care. At 7 seconds, gentle sip.

Premium cafe environment visible in soft-focus background.
35mm lens, shallow depth of field, creamy bokeh on background.
Commercial product photography quality.

Sound effects: Cup against saucer, quiet sip.
Ambient: Espresso machine, quiet cafe murmur.

No text overlay, no subtitles."
```

### Tech Product

```bash
nano-banana --video "Slow orbit shot rotating around a smartphone.

A sleek modern smartphone rests on a minimalist white surface.
Camera smoothly rotates 45 degrees around the device over 8 seconds,
revealing the thin profile and premium materials. Screen displays
a colorful abstract wallpaper.

Studio lighting: Key light from top-left creating subtle shadows,
soft fill from right, rim light from behind.

Clean white infinity curve background. Apple-style commercial aesthetic.
No sound, pure visual focus.

No text, no UI elements, no subtitles."
```

## Action Sequences

```bash

An athletic woman in her 30s, black running gear, ponytail, focused expression,
jogs along a cliffside trail. Camera moves parallel at her pace.

```

## Emotional Moments

### Intimate Conversation

```bash
nano-banana --video "Static shot, tight two-shot composition.

Two friends sit across from each other at a small cafe table.
Woman on left, brown hair, says: 'I've made my decision.'
Woman on right listens intently, slight nod of understanding at 6 seconds.

Warm cafe interior, soft window light from left, candles on table.
Intimate, meaningful atmosphere. Independent film aesthetic.

Ambient: Quiet cafe sounds, soft jazz in background.

Emotional, naturalistic performance. No subtitles."
```

### Revelation Moment

```bash
nano-banana --video "Slow push-in (dolly-in) on character's face.

A man in his 50s, weathered features, gray stubble, sits alone.
As camera slowly moves closer, his expression shifts from confusion
to dawning realization. Eyes widen slightly at 5 seconds.
At 7 seconds, a single tear forms.

Dimly lit room, single practical lamp creating dramatic side lighting.
Emotional close-up, 85mm lens, shallow depth of field.

Sound: Ticking clock, distant rain, swelling emotional strings.

Powerful, understated performance. No subtitles, no text."
```

## Transitional Shots

### Time Passage

```bash
nano-banana --video "Static shot of a window with changing light.

A large window frame, plants on the sill, city view beyond.
Light transitions from warm morning golden tones through bright
midday to warm evening amber over 8 seconds, suggesting time passing.

Timelapse-style lighting shift. Shadows move across the floor.
Contemplative, peaceful atmosphere.

Ambient: City sounds shifting subtly, birds to traffic to evening quiet.

Minimalist, artistic. No subtitles."
```

### Scene Bridge

```bash
nano-banana --video "Dolly-out revealing location change.

Camera starts tight on a coffee cup, pulls back over 8 seconds
to reveal the cup is on an airplane tray table. Window shows
clouds outside. Business traveler visible in seat, looking out window.

Airplane cabin ambient lighting, natural light from window.
Travel documentary feel.

Sound effects: Airplane engine hum, quiet cabin ambiance, ice in cup.

Smooth transition shot. No subtitles."
```
